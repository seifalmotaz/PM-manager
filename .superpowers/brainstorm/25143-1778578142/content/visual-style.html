Define the Visual Aesthetic

Which "vibe" best fits your vision for a modern, enterprise-grade PM tool?

Slate & Steel

"The Slate"

Muted dark tones (slate/zinc), crisp borders, high-contrast text. Minimalist but feels heavy and reliable. (Inspired by Linear/Raycast)

Azure Soft

"The Professional"

Clean whites and soft blues. Subtle depth with shadows. Rounded corners. Feels friendly but robust. (Modern SaaS standard)

B&W Ultra

"The Minimalist"

Strictly black and white. Ultra-thin borders. No shadows. Focus is 100% on the data. (Swiss design aesthetic)

Dark Mode Support

Should the redesign support native Dark Mode? (Highly recommended for developers/PMs who work late).

Yes, Dark Mode is a must.
